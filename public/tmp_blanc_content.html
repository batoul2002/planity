Easily accessed via Bekfaya’s main road, Blanc De Chene comfortably accommodates up to 200 guests indoors and 500 outdoors. Upon arrival, their professional valet service will safely park the vehicles in a designated spot, and your guests will then be escorted to an elegantly seated welcome area with live piano performance, for them to enjoy their welcome drinks.

All throughout the evening, their tailored and highly amusing musical performances, in addition to their superb catering services will then guarantee that your guests will enjoy a special dinner setting with personalized culinary delights.

With a charming surrounding panoramic view, the enchanting greenery around, the many scenic spots on location, and just the perfect breeze during Lebanon’s summer heat, your dream wedding at Blanc De Chene will certainly be magical.

Some of the facilities offered by the venue:

  • Event/Wedding coordination
  • 1 Hostess for every 50 guests
  • Fully furnished welcome drink area
  • Floral wall of fame
  • Live piano performance (in the welcome drink area)
  • 2 LCD’s installed in the Welcome Drink area & a Large LED screen in the ceremony area
  • Fake cake, real cake, and volcanoes
  • Black-wood dance floor (anti slip)
  • Full lighting and sound systems
  • DJ’s
  • And many other services…

Blanc De Chene owns its own catering services. You have the possibility to choose between a Lebanese Seated Dinner, a Set Menu, or an International Buffet.

When we visited the venue, we met the owner and manager Mr Jospeph Sayah, he was very welcoming and he explained to us what makes his venue unique. We sensed the secret of Blanc De Chene which is a high level of professionalism that turns your wedding into a unique unforgettable experience.
