Exquisitely located at the edge of the sea, and at the most prominent spot of Byblos Harbor, Byblos Sur Mer hotel provides privacy and serenity in a truly charming setting and offers its guests the comforts of a home at the heart of a legend.
Byblos Sur Mer overlooks the ancient Byblos port and castle on one side, and a panoramic view of the Mediterranean sea on the other, in a carefully decorated space furnished with local art and artisanal wares, ancient relics, and precious items from private collections.
