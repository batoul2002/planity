Directions

From the airport, take the highway towards the city centre.
Upon arriving to downtown, make a left towards Martyrs’ Square.
At the end of the road, turn left onto Weygand Street.
Pass Al Nahar building on the right. Continue straight to the end of the street and make a right towards the sea.
Keep going till end of the way, St Georges to the right side, Blom Bank to the left, then take the first left just by the Palm Beach Hotel.
Once out of the exit, continue straight and Phoenicia Hotel is to the right side.
